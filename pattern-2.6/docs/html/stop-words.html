Stop words

Stop words are words that are so common that they are often filtered out prior to, or after, processing of natural language data (text). For example, the pattern.vector module (by default) ignores stop words when constructing bag-of-words.

There is no definitive list of stop words. The following set is based on Martin Porter's list and expanded with words that occur frequently in other lists:
